Swedish Meatballs

Swedish meatballs, a classic comfort food, are known for their savory flavor and creamy sauce. This traditional Swedish dish has gained popularity worldwide for its rich taste and simple preparation.

With just a few ingredients and some basic cooking techniques, you can recreate this beloved dish at home. Serve Swedish meatballs as an appetizer, a main course, or even as a party snack.

Prep time

15 min.

Cook time

30 min.

Servings

4 servings

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Meatballs: In a large bowl, mix together ground beef, breadcrumbs, egg, onion, salt, pepper, and allspice until well combined. Shape the mixture into small meatballs.
  2. Cook the Meatballs: In a skillet over medium heat, add oil and brown the meatballs on all sides, about 5-7 minutes. Once browned, remove the meatballs from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Make the Sauce: In the same skillet, melt butter and whisk in flour to create a roux. Gradually pour in beef broth and heavy cream, stirring constantly until the sauce thickens. Season with salt, pepper, and Worcestershire sauce.
  4. Combine: Return the meatballs to the skillet with the sauce. Simmer for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the meatballs are cooked through and the sauce has thickened.
  5. Serve: Garnish the Swedish meatballs with chopped parsley and serve hot over mashed potatoes, noodles, or with lingonberry sauce on the side.

Ingredients:

  1. 1 lb ground beef
  2. 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  3. 1 egg
  4. 1/4 cup onion, finely chopped
  5. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  6. 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  7. 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
  8. 2 tablespoons oil
