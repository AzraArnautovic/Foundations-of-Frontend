California Rolls Sushi

California Rolls are a popular type of sushi roll featuring imitation crab, avocado, and cucumber wrapped in seaweed and sushi rice. Despite their name, California Rolls actually originated in North America and are known for their light and refreshing flavors.

With just a few ingredients and a little practice, you can create delicious California Rolls at home to enjoy as a snack or part of a sushi dinner.

Prep time

25 min.

Assemble

15 min.

Servings

4 servings

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Gather sushi rice, nori seaweed sheets, imitation crab, avocado, cucumber, soy sauce, and wasabi. Slice the avocado and cucumber into thin strips.
  2. Cook the Rice: Rinse sushi rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Cook the rice according to package instructions, then let it cool to room temperature.
  3. Assemble the Rolls: Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat. Moisten your hands with water and spread a thin layer of rice over the nori, leaving about a half-inch border at the top. Arrange the crab, avocado, and cucumber in the center of the rice.
  4. Roll the Sushi: Starting from the bottom, roll the sushi tightly using the bamboo mat. Apply gentle pressure as you roll to ensure the ingredients stick together. Seal the edge of the nori with a little water.
  5. Slice and Serve: Use a sharp knife to slice the roll into bite-sized pieces. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger on the side. Enjoy your homemade California Rolls!

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup sushi rice
  2. 4 nori seaweed sheets
  3. 4 oz imitation crab
  4. 1 avocado
  5. 1/2 cucumber
  6. Soy sauce, for serving
  7. Wasabi, for serving
  8. Pickled ginger, for serving
