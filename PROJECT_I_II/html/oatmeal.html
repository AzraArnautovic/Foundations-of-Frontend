Oatmeal

Oatmeal is a nutritious and easy-to-make breakfast option that is both delicious and healthy. With just a few simple ingredients and minimal cooking time, you can enjoy a hearty and satisfying meal to start your day.

Oatmeal is packed with fiber, vitamins, and minerals, making it an ideal choice for a balanced breakfast. Plus, it's highly customizable, allowing you to add your favorite toppings for added flavor and nutrition.

Prep time

5 min.

Cook time

5 min.

Servings

1 serving

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Oatmeal: In a small saucepan, bring water to a boil. Stir in oats and reduce heat to low. Cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the oatmeal is thick and creamy.
  2. Serve: Transfer the cooked oatmeal to a bowl and add your favorite toppings such as fresh fruits, nuts, seeds, honey, or maple syrup. Enjoy your nutritious and delicious oatmeal!

Ingredients:

  1. 1/2 cup rolled oats
  2. 1 cup water or milk
  3. Optional toppings: fresh fruits, nuts, seeds, honey, maple syrup, etc.
